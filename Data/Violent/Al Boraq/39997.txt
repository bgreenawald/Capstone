The calls and help expressions from the forgotten Chechnya which suffer from the Russian occupation and oppression have reached us .So its was obligatory on us to form a a standing and to start a public action and to provide support to the forgotten Chechen people As Allah , The Most Exalted , said "but if they seek your help in religion, it is your duty to help them " Therefore the decision was to announce the establishment of " The Last Call campaign " , the public campaign that provides solidarity with the Chechen issue , the campaign that supports heroes of the Chechen Jihad . This Islamic Public Campaign wasn't established except after the World neglected the Chechen issue and Russia committed massacres against the young Muslim people under international silence and negligence of the issue .
