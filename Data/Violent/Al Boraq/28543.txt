27-4 The Islamic army in Iraq\ The eighth release - Absheree Dar As-Salam In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful. Fight them! Allah will chastise them at your hands, and He will lay them low and give you victory over them, and He will heal the breasts of folk who are believers Praise be to Allah, the All- Strong, the All-Mighty. Peace and blessings be upon the prophet of guidance, the prophet of the great battle, all-Mighty on his family and all his companions Allah helped your brothers in the media committee in the Islamic army in Iraq in finalizing the eighth release (Absheree Dar As-Salam) which comes as a message clarifying that the Mujahideen won’t leave the Islamic caliphate capital and won’t let the infidels or the hypocrites or the traitors change the Islamic culture of Baghdad. We promise that we will bring the peace again to Baghdad. For the first time, the release includes new Nasheeds about the Jihad in Iraq in general and some special Nasheeds for the Islamic army. . We ask Allah to accept this release and give us victory upon his enemies.
