In response to the initiative of the "Union Tree" launched by HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the UAE Vice President, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, HH Dr Hamdan Musallam al Mazrouei, Awqaf's chairman, planted tree in the courtyard of the Sheikh Sultan bin Zayed Mosque in Abu Dhabi
On this occasion, Dr al Mazrouei praised the keen interest of the wise UAE leadership in preserving the Union achievements, promoting cooperation, compassion and harmony amongst members of the community and raising the status of the national values and principles established by the founding fathers. He also prayed for the souls of the late Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan, the founding of this nation, may All Allah have mercy on him, Sheikh Rashid bin Saeed Al Maktoum, and all Their Brothers, the late UAE Sheikhs.
Dr al Mazrouei supplicated to Allah to grant success to the UAE President, Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, may Allah protect him, HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the UAE Vice President, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, Their Highness The Rulers of the Emirates and HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces
He added that Awqaf is keen to implement all initiatives and directives by the leadership and promote the Union values, cooperation, loyalty and love for the nation amongst citizens.
