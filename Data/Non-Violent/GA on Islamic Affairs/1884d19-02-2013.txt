To cope with the urban expansion in the new developments and in line with its strategic plan adopted by the Cabinet, Awqaf signed a contract with Al Nakheel Contracting and General Maintenance Company to build five mosques in the new Al Falah area. Each mosque will accommodate 300 worshippers and will be completed within next months.
In a statement, HE Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, Awqaf's director general, said the UAE wise leadership is keen to establish enough mosques to meet the needs of population in new residential areas along with other public facilities and services.
On this occasion Dr al Kaabi praised the unlimited support being offered to Awqaf by HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, may Allah protect him, his Deputy HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, Their Highness Members of the Supreme Council, the Rulers of the Emirates, and HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces. He also highlighted the invaluable support of Abu Dhabi Executive Council, which is keen to follow up the implementation of mosque projects.
