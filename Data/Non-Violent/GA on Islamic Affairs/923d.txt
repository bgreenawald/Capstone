The department undertakes the testing and selection of qualified imams and mu'ezzins to work in GAIAE mosques as well as government and private mosques.




Department

Preaching



Service

Select imams and mue'zzins for UAE mosques



Service Status

Active



Type of Service

Action needed



Target audience

Imams and Mue'zzins



Service Accessibility

Interested candidates must forward their CVs via fax or in the mail. They may also submit it by hand to the section of employees affairsat GAIAE main office



Processing time

30 days

Required Documents

Passport copy
Diploma in Islamic law (Sharia) approved by UAE Ministry of Higher Education



Terms and Conditions


Candidate  who does  not hold a diploma in Islamic law must have memorized the Holy Quran
Must be more than 35 years old
Must be married

None
