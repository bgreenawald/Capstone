As part of its strategic partnerships with the Holy Quran Radio Abu Dhabi (ADHQR) and in recognition of the latter's role in promoting religious awareness in society, Awqaf has recently honoured its working team.
This came during a ceremony held at Awqaf headquarters in Abu Dhabi, where HE Dr Hamdan Musallam al Mazrouei received Mr Abdul Rahman Awadh al Harthi, the executive director of Abu Dhabi Radio Network (ADRN), Mr Abdullah al Zaabi, ADRN's deputy executive director, Mr Abdul Rahman al Tunaiji, ADHQR' director, and the broadcasting staff. The delegation toured the Official Fatwa Centre and watched a documentary film on Awqaf achievements under the Union.
In a speech, Dr al Mazrouei highlighted the continuous support to Awqaf from HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, may Allah protect him, his deputy HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, their brothers Their Highness Rulers of the Emirates, his trustworthy Crown Prince, HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces.
He also praised the leadership's keen interest in promoting religious awareness based on principles of moderation and enhancing virtues amongst members of the community.
On another note, Dr al Mazroeui lauded the fruitful collaboration between Awqaf and ADHQR as well as various national media outlets in the country.
For his part, Mr al Harthi commended the efforts being made by Awqaf in promoting religious, national and human values in the society. He said, "We are glad to receive this recognition. It is the outcome of the cultural coordination between Awqaf and media in general and the Holy Quran Radio in particular in accordance to the directives of the wise leadership."
