The Department of Research and Censorship is under the authority of GAIAE executive director for Islamic affairs. It was established pursuant to the cabinet decree no. 22 for the year 2009. The Department undertakes the responsibilities of writing and publishing its own research and publications in addition to monitoring all religious books, research and studies. Strategic Goal:• Enhance awareness of Islamic culture. • Protect society from the impact of destructive ideas.• Emphasize the true essence of Islam as a religion of moderation.• Promote Islamic tolerance.• Issue books, electronic or audio-visual materials and monitor all religious publications in the UAE.The department is composed of two sections: research section and religious publications censorship section.
