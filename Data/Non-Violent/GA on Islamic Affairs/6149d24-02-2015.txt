HE Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, Awqaf's Chairman, stressed that the creation of a new position of Chief Executive Officer of Innovation, announced by HH Sheikh Mansour bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Presidential Affairs, is a strategic initiative that comes as part of the UAE wise leadership's march towards excellence, development and modernisation.
"This drive is led by HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Supreme Deputy Commander of the UAE Armed Forces, and Their Highnesses Members of the Supreme Council the Rulers of the Emirates," Dr al Kaabi said, noting that the initiative will next mark all aspects of functioning at government entities.
"As a federal government institution, Awqaf's top priority is to keep pace with all the latest developments and meet the UAE leadership's keen aspirations in creating environment that is conducive to innovation for each Emirati," he underlined.
"Indeed, it is a leading move of a worldwide dimension. It is expected to bring in great results benefiting every citizen and resident in the UAE. Furthermore, it will increase the outcomes of investment in modern technologies and smart applications of which the country has become a leader," Dr al Kaabi concluded.
