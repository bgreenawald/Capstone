Awqaf has appointed 192 outstanding Holy Quran tutors after receiving training aimed at developing their personal skills to help them carry out their tasks.
This came as part of Awqaf strategic plan and in the implementation of the UAE prudent leadership's vision to attract male and female citizens to undertake tutorship roles in Quran memorisation centres.
HE Dr Mohammed Matar Al Kaabi, Awqaf Chairman, said the entity is proceeding in accordance with its strategic plans to establish more centres and increase the number of memorisation sessions, adding that Awqaf is also interested in developing the skills of administrative and teaching staff.
Dr al Kaabi appreciates the UAE prudent leadership's interest in teaching  Quran and instilling its values and instructions in the hearts of the youth He also praised the great support to Awqaf and its Quran memorisation centres from HH Sheikh Khalifa Bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, HH Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, HH Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces, and Their Highnesses Rulers of the Emirates, who followed in the footsteps of the founding leader Sheikh Zayed Bin Sultan Al Nahyan, who laid the foundations for the Holy Quran memorisation centres in the UAE.
Dr Al Kaabi explained that this step came as part of the special interest by Awqaf in Emirati talents who successfully completed Quran memorisation training, noting that they would be employed to teach Quran at the official Quran memorisation centres. This way, he explained, a generation of memorisers would form, one that masters its sciences, adopts its morals, possesses virtuous qualities and be loyal to their country and wise leadership.
He added that the Quranic centres receive growing interest from male and female students, who are keen to learn Quran and who believe in the role it plays in refining eloquence, rhetoric, and good manners for the good of the individual, family and society.
