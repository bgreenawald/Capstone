Awqaf announced it had achieved a customer satisfaction rate of 87 per cent regarding the services it provides to the community, according to a field study conducted by the Awqaf's Office of Strategic Planning in collaboration with an independent opinion poll institution.
HE Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, Awqaf's Chairman, affirmed that the great financial and moral support received by Awqaf from HH Sheikh Khalifa Bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, HH Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, HH Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces, and Their Highnesses the Supreme Council Members, the Rulers of the Emirates, was behind the progress Awqaf had achieved in all areas. This helped it to achieve high rates of satisfaction and receive many certificates of achievement and appreciation at the local and regional levels.
The study explored the customers' opinions about Awqaf's contribution to promote religious awareness and Islamic culture and nurture the values of moderation and tolerance in the society.
While the average satisfaction with Awqaf's services reached 87 per cent, satisfaction of those dealing with the Holy Quran memorisation centres touched 84.37 per cent, waqf tenants 89.05 per cent, mosque-goers 81.14 per cent, Fatwa questioners 81.86 per cent, donors and Waqf givers 92.07 per cent, pilgrims 90.81 per cent and campaign operators 87.02 per cent.
