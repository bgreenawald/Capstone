The Ras Al Khaimah Charity Foundation has supported the local imams of mosques in the emirate with AED 317,000 that will benefit 317 Imams.
"The Foundation is keenly supporting the destitute and continent members of the society in line with the directives and vision of the UAE's wise leadership in the field of charitable and humanitarian work," said Mohammed Jakka al Mansouri, the foundation's Secretary General.
He pointed out that giving this amount for the third time in a row reflects the Foundation's strive to support the imams of mosques and enhance its communication with a whole range of entities and foundations.
For his part, Ali Rashid al Khanbouli, Director of Awqaf's branch in Ras Al Khaimah, said the continual support of Ras Al Khaimah Charity Foundation for all continent categories of the society is in line with the directives of HH Sheikh Saud Bin Saqr al Qasimi, Ruler of Ras Al Khaimah, who encourages charitable and humanitarian work and provides the necessary support for it.
