Awqaf in the Western Region honours the ideal employee and worker of the first quarter of this year. The ceremony was attended by Mr Sultan Zayed al Mazrouei, the branch director, and Nasser Khalfan al Mazrouei, head of Islamic Affairs.
The award for the best worker went to Batani Mohammed Nurallah, the best Imam to  Mohammed Mahfooth, and the best employee to Mohammed Jamal Ahmed Hashim.
At the opening of the ceremony, Mr Sultan al Mazrouei prayed to Allah to sustain HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed al Nahyan, the UAE President, may Allah protect him, his Deputy HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the UAE Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, Their Highness Members of the Supreme Council, the Rulers of the Emirates, HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces, and HH Sheikh Hamdan bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the Ruler's Representative in the Western Region,  with good health and wellness.
Mr al Mazrouei mentioned that this award should motivate all members of staff because many deserve it also. Wishing them success next time, he urged everyone to work hard and excel in their performance.
