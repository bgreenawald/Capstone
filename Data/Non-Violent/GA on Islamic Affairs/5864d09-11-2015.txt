In a periodic meeting held in Abu Dhabi to follow up on the status of Sheikh Zayed Mosque in Jordan, Awqaf is to continue overseeing the mosque's affairs.
The coordination meeting was headed by Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, Awqaf's Chairman, in presence of Mohammed Obeid al Mazrouei, Executive Director for Islamic Affairs, and Sultan al Niyadi, Director of Mosques Department. From the Jordanian side, the meeting was attended by the Imam of the mosque and the director of services department that manages the mosque security, cleaning and maintenance.
It is worth mentioning that the Sheikh Zayed Mosque has provided around four thousand Iftar meals on daily basis during the last holy month of Ramadan.
