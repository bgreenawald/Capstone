Awqaf signed a contract yesterday to construct a three-story building in Sharjah with a cost of AED 4,950,000 with Obaid al Abdi Contracting. The contract was signed at Awqaf's headquarters in Abu Dhabi in the framework of developing its endowment resources in accordance with the latest investment procedures.
The contract was signed by Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, Director General of Awqaf, and Abdul Rahman Khalil, General Manager of the contracting company, in the presence of Khalid al Neyadi, Executive Director of Waqf Affairs.
The construction will be funded from the revenue of Awqaf's endowment projects. Dr al Kaabi clarified that the signing of this contract crowned the efforts of Awqaf to expand various endowment projects, including buildings, shops, malls, and villas.
He praised the material and moral support given to Awqaf by HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, may Allah protect him; HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai; Their Highnesses Members of the Supreme Council Rulers of the Emirates and HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces.
