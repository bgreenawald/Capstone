Awqaf has completed the furnishing of 485 mosques all over the UAE. The move is part of an ongoing project by Awqaf to cater for the needs of mosques as per the directives of the prudent UAE leadership, HE Mohammed Obeid al Mazrouei, the executive director of Islamic affairs said, adding that for this purpose Awqaf had chosen the finest carpets to help worshippers perform their prayers in comfort and serenity.
On this occasion, Mr al Mazrouei praised the keen interest in mosques by  HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, may Allah protect him, HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the UAE Vice President, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, Their Highness Rulers of the Emirates, Members of the Supreme Court, and HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the Armed Force.
