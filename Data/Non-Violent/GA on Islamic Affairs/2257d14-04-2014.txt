HE Dr Hamdan Musallam al Mazrouei, Awqaf's chairman, has inaugurated Rashid bin Humaila al Mazrouei Mosque in Madinat Zayed in the Western Region. Present were Mr Sultan Zayed al Mazrouei, director of Awqaf's branch in the Western Region, in addition to members from the local community.
On this occasion, Dr Hamdan al Mazrouei praised the keen interest to mosques by HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, may Allah protect him, HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the UAE Vice President, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, their brothers, Their Highness the Rulers of the Emirates and HH the General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces.
He also commended the close follow-up of HH Sheikh Hamdan bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the Ruler's Representative in the Western Region as well as the UAE's wise directives with regard to promoting the role of mosques in society, which benefit members of the community and highlight the moderate principles of Islam and its civilisational image.
Dr al Mazrouei further lauded the positive interaction of philanthropists to Awqaf's charity initiatives.
For his part, Mr Sulatan al Mazrouei, said that the new mosque accommodated more than 1500 worshippers and fitted with all necessary amenities. It features an architectural design that combines the Islamic tradition and modern style. It is part of a plan to build a number of mosques across the Western Region cities and places where there is a need in line with the wise leadership's instructions.
