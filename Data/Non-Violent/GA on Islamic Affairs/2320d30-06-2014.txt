HH Sheikh Rashid bin Saud bin Rashid Al Mualla, Crown Prince of Umm Al Quwain, has recently received Dr Mohammed Matar Al Kaabi, Awqaf's Director General, who was accompanied by executive and branches' directors. HH Sheikh Rashid was briefed by the director general on the readiness of mosques to receive worshippers during Ramadan, especially for Tarawih prayers and Ramadan lectures as well as other special lectures to be delivered by Scholars-Guests of the UAE President. Awqaf will highlight the role of national service in the community.
Sheikh Rashid expressed his appreciation for Awqaf's efforts in promoting the true values of religion in society and in serving mosques to highlight their status and mission. He also stressed the importance of safeguarding UAE's authentic traditions and customs. 
Sheikh Rashid listened to a review about Awqaf efforts in the Emirate of Umm Al Quwain.
Sheikh Rashid lauded the keen interest of the wise leadership in Islamic affairs and the continuous support of HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, may Allah protect him, and Their Highnesses the Supreme Council Members, the Rulers of the Emirates, to the programmes carried out by the General Authority of Islamic Affairs and Endowments.
These projects have received immense admiration from everyone as they serve members of the community and guide them to adhere to their religion and culture and respect the values and customs of the UAE society.  The Crown Prince received the new version of the book "Lessons of Mosques" from the Director General of Awqaf. The book is a compilation of religious lectures to be delivered in mosques after Al Asr prayer across UAE.
The meeting was attended by a number of Sheikhs, along with a host of senior officials.
