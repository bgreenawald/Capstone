Department

Religious Centers and Institutes



Service

Secure male and female teachers of the Holy Quran to government and private institutions and authorities



Service Status

Active



Type of Service

Action needed



Target audience


Government bodies and authorities

The government or private authority must submit a written request to GAIAE department of religious centers and institutes. The department secures highly qualified teachers
