Awqaf has recently concluded its summer internship for Emirati students. The training programme was approved by the Board of Directors with the aim of training 100 Emirati students at Awqaf's branches all over the country during the school summer holidays.
The programme reflects Awqaf's keen efforts to engage school students in its activities and events that serve the community. It run from July 5 through August 6, 2015 and included several academic and hands-on cultural and educational training courses. Students were awarded incentives of AED 2000 each at the end of the programme.
