HH Sheikh Saud bin Saqr Al Qasimi, Supreme Council Member and Ruler of Ras Al Khaimah, received Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, the Director General Awqaf, at his palace yesterday. The executives and directors of departments and branches of Awqaf across the country were also present at the meeting.
During the meeting, HE Al Qasimi praised the efforts exerted by Awqaf in terms of professional development and performance improvement, thus empowering it to continue its national strategic achievements and realise its mission, vision, objectives, and projects to serve the houses of Allah. HH Al Qasimi was briefed by Dr al Kaabi on Awqaf's efforts in RAK in maintaining and cleaning the mosques, as well as the development of endowment resources to keep in pace with the urban boom and accelerated development witnessed by the emirate thanks to the continuous support of His Highness.
Dr al Kaabi hailed the constant care given to the houses of Allah by the UAE wise leadership and to mosque construction,  those being the beacons of guidance, worship, learning and knowledge in the Muslim community.
