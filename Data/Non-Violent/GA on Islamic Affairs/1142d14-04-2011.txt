The General Authority for Islamic Affairs and Endowments (GAIAE) on Wednesday (4/13) honored the Eminent Islamic scholars who lectured at the training seminar for imams and khutaba (orator of the Friday's sermon). The seminar, which was held in Al Ain, aimed at enhancing their religious knowledge and public speaking skills.
Mr.Mohammed Obeid AlMazrouei, who presented the certificates of appreciations to the Islamic scholars, said that GAIAE is eager to improve the performance of all imams. He added that this training course is a step towards fulfilling GAIAE strategic plan. He expressed his thanks and appreciation to the Islamic scholars who relayed their distinguished knowledge and intensive experience.
Mr.AlMazrouei was pleased with the spirit of competition between imams and khutaba, which will prompt GAIAE to offer them the best methodolody to improve their public speaking skills, noting that Friday prayer is of vital importance to GAIAE.
