Awqaf took part in the events orgainsed by the Abu Dhabi Centre for Autism of the Zayed Higher Organisation for Humanitarian Care and Special Needs as part of the World Autism Awareness Month.
Events were held under the theme "Together we make a difference" and included handing out gifts to the children and their parents. 
On this occasion, Awqaf stressed the need to give much care to those with special needs in order to develop their skills and talents in line with the teachings of Islam and the directives of the UAE's wise leadership meet their requirements and offering them an environment conducive to expanding their perceptions and helping them demonstrate their capabilities.
