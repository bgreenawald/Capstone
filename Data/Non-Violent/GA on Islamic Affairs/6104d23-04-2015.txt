HE Dr Mohammed Matar Salem al Kaabi, Awqaf's Chairman, presided over the meeting of the heads of committees of the UAE President HH Sheikh Khalifa Bin Zayed Al Nahyan's guest scholars' programme for observing the days and nights of the Holy Month of Ramadan.
The meeting, which was held at the Awqaf headquarters in Abu Dhabi, discussed the executive plan for this year programme (1436 AH-2015 AD), being supervised by the Ministry of Presidential Affairs and implemented by Awqaf.
At the beginning of the meeting, Dr al Kaabi praised this boon from HH Sheikh Khalifa which enriched the atmospheres of the holy month of Ramadan with lessons and sermons, praying to Allah, the Almighty, to bestow health and wellness upon him and have mercy on Sheikh Zayed Bin Sultan Al Nahyan, may Allah rest his soul in peace.
Dr al Kaabi underlined Awqaf's keen interest in upgrading this programme in coordination with the Ministry of Presidential Affairs and laying down programmes and executive plans with the aim of meeting the expectations of the UAE's wise leadership.
The meeting discussed various steps required to implement the programme together with a number of points relating to coordination and communication with the concerned entities and stakeholders.
It also discussed other matters related to booking theatres to hold conferences, seminars and Ramadan evenings as well as preparing the daily programme, the titles of the lessons given in mosques, the programme of Ramadan Majlises and the media plan of the programme.
