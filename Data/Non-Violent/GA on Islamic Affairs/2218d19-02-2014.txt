HH Sheikh Hamad bin Mohammed Al Sharqi, Supreme Council Member and Ruler of Fujairah, has received HE Dr Hamdan Musallam Al Mazrouei, Awqaf's Chairman, HE Dr Mohammed Matar Al Kaabi, Director General and Mohammad Obaid Al Mazrouei, Islamic Affairs' Executive Director.
During the meeting, Sheikh Hamad was briefed on Awqaf's plans, programmes and strategy in the next phase all over the UAE and especially in the Emirate of Fujairah.
Sheikh Hamad listened to  a detailed explanation  about Awqaf's strategy and the progress of projects under way, which it was carrying out in the Emirate of Fujairah, such as the Waqf building and mosques.
Awqaf's officials praised the support of HH the Ruler of Fujairah and his keen interest in following the implementation of these projects and facilitating the procedures. They also commended his wise guidance and innovative ideas given by him in this regard.
The meeting was attended by a number of Sheikhs and officials .
