HH Sheikh Humaid bin Rashid Al Nuaimi, Member of the Supreme Council, Ruler of Ajman, has inaugurated Abdullah bin Ghlita Mosque in Al Helou area.
The ceremony was attended by Mr Obeid Hamad al Zaabi, Awqaf's branch director in Ajman, Mr Hamad Abdullah bin Ghlita, HH the Ruler's personal secretary and a host of local departments' directors and dignitaries.
HH Sheikh Humaid praised the special support of HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, and his brothers, Their Highness the Rulers of the Emirates, may Allah protect them all. He also expressed his appreciation of UAE citizens' keen interest in building and maintaining mosques, and highlighted Awqaf's efforts in this regard.
For his part, Mr al Zaabi hailed the invaluable care and support Awqaf is receiving from HH Sheikh Khalifa, his Deputy Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, Their Highness Members of the Supreme Council, Rulers of the Emirates and HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces.
He mentioned that Awqaf was devoting great care to building mosques according to designs that are convivial to the environment and fitting them with the latest audio technology, fine carpets and facilities for disabled and the elderly.
The new mosque is located in a strategic place on the road linking Ajman Emirate with Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed and Emirates Roads. It occupies a space of 730 sqm with a capacity of 700 worshippers. It has facilities such as women prayer hall, an accommodation for Imam, a library, toilets, ablution area, special exit for disabled and car parking lots.
