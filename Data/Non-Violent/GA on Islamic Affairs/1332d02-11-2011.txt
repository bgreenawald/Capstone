Requests to build new mosques are received by the department which communicates with the Municipality for land allocation and issuance of  building permits





Department

Mosques Administration



Service

Requests for allocating pieces of land and issuance of building permits for new mosques



Service Status

Active



Type of Service


Action needed 




Target audience


All members of the community




Service Accessibility


To apply for a mosque-construction permit , please click on Mosque-construction permit request and fill out the form. Please specify which Awqaf's branch you would like to coordinate with for this purpose




 Processing time


 We cannot determine the processing duration as the completion of the service depends on the input from other government bodies
