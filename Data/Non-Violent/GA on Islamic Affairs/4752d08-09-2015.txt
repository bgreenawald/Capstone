HE Dr Mohammed Matar Al Kaabi, Awqaf Chairman, received HE Prof Mohammed Mokhtar Gomaa, the Egyptian minister of Awqaf, who visited Awqaf headoffice in Abu Dhabi to discuss aspects of mutual cooperation between the two sides and learn about Awqaf's experience in managing religious affairs in the UAE.
Dr Al Kaabi welcomed the Egyptian minister and stressed the importance of the visit that highlighted the importance of the cooperation and exchange of experiences between the two countries in the management of religious matters. Dr Al Kaabi briefed Prof Gomaa on the progress made by Awqaf under the continuous support of HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces, and Their Highnesses Rulers of the Emirates.
Al Kaabi also highlighted UAE's leadership's vision in promoting the concepts of moderation and tolerance and establishing a unified source for Fatwa, demonstrating Awqaf's excellence in realizing its mission and vision.
For his part, the Egyptian minister commended on the hospitality shown to him and praised Awqaf's excellence in offering its services to the public, urging similar religious entities to benefit from such a unique and modern experience.
