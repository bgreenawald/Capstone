Awqaf's director general, HE Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, received here at Awqaf headquarters in Abu Dhabi the Vice Minister of foreign affairs of Italy. The Italian official was accompanied by a delegation, including the Italian head of mission in UAE.
Dr al Kaabi welcomed the delegation and praised the deep friendly relations between the leaders of the two countries and the areas of mutual cooperation. Both sides discussed ways of promoting collaboration in religious field.
The Italian delegation lauded the smart use of technology in public services and commended the religious tolerance and coexistence prevailing in UAE. They also highlighted the respect which all religious groups in cities and labour communities are enjoying in UAE.
