Service performed in one of the following cases
Contractor decides to resume his work after an interruption period (with no violations)                                                                                                                   Three years have passed after the Higher Committee's decision to freeze his work permit





Department

Hajj and Umrah



Service


Reissue a permit for Hajj and Umrah contractors




Service Status

Active



Type of Service

Action needed



Target audience

Hajj and Umra campaign operators   



Service Accessibility


 
Via GAIAE website www.awqaf.gov.ae  .Then click on Reissue a permit for Hajj and Umrah contractors form

Required Documents


4 personal photos
Copies of passport and civil registry
Certificate of good conduct
Certificate of good health
No objection letter from the employer
 
 




Terms and Conditions


 
Be UAE citizen
Must not be employed by GAIAE
Must be between 25-70 years old
Must have a rented office
Must pay the fees for all the period of interruption based on the class assigned to him
 




Fees


 
Outstanding class: 10.000 dirhams
First class            :   7.000 dirhams
Second class        :   5.000 dirhams
Third class           :   3.000 dirham
 




Service Location

GAIAE main office in Abu Dhabi
