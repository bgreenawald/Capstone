HE Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, Awqaf's Chairman, received yesterday at Abu Dhabi international airport HE Dr Mohammed Mokhtar Gomaa, Egypt's Minister of Religious Endowments (Awqaf).
On the occasion, Dr al Kaabi said that the visit enhances the distinguished relations between the two wise leaderships of the UAE and Egypt.
"The visit is also in line with the continued co-operation and co-ordination between the Egyptian Ministry of Religious Endowments (Awqaf) and UAE's Awqaf," Dr al Kaabi concluded.
