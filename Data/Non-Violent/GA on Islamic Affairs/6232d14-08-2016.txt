HE Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, Chairman of Awqaf, stated that in collaboration with the Abu Dhabi Holy Quran radio station, the most popular state radio station in the UAE, recorded 80 radio episodes about the Hajj rituals and virtues.
The episodes covered a range of topics, including: Hajj legacy and lessons, Answering Allah's call, The Hajj mission, Hajj verses and guidance, Glimmers from the Hajj of the prophet PBUH - Fiqh (Islamic Jurisprudence) of women in Hajj and the daily broadcast "Yastaftoonak" (Asking for Fatwas), which will be aired starting the 1st of Dhul Hijjah.
The episodes were prepared and recorded by a group of the most prominent male and female preachers of the Awqaf. The radio will start airing these educational topics starting the 20th of the current month - Dhu al-Qidah, during the preparation phase for travelling to the holy sites.
