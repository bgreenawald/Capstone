Awqaf would like to announce the beginning of registration and enrolment in the summer semester of Quran memorisation in more than 198 mosques (345 sessions in mosques) and a number of Quran memorisation centres. The sessions will be conducted from 4pm to 7pm starting from Sunday, July 2 through Thursday, July 27, 2017.
The move came in response to the interest shown by parents in investing the school summer holidays of their sons and daughters in activities that are conducive to develop their talents related to Quran recitation, memorisation and Tafseer (interpretation). It is also aimed at allowing the opportunity for the students to complete memorising the whole Quran.
Awqaf pointed out that transportation have been arranged for the students' travel to and from the memorisation locations.
Concerning the categories targeted by the initiative, Awqaf highlighted that it included two categories: male and female students aged between 6 to 18 years and the category of adults above 18 years of age. It underlined that the public can learn about the location of mosques, centres and sessions via its website www.awqaf.gov.ae or at the Quranic centres.
