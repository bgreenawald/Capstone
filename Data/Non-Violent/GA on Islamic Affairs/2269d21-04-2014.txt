HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Hamad bin Mohammed Al Sharqi, Crown Prince of Fujairah, Chairman of Fujairah Foundation for Region Development (FFRD), received HE Dr Hamdan Musallam al Mazrouei, Awqaf's Chairman, who came to greet him. 
During the meeting, which took place at the Ruler's Court in Fujairah, both sides discussed ways to promote cooperation and partnership in community service. On this occasion, Islamic Affairs donated one million dirham to FFRD in support for its social activities in the Emirate.
The meeting was attended by Mr Saeed Mohammed al Raqbani, FFRD's Vice President, HE Salem al Zahmi, Crown Prince's Office manager and FFRD's board of directors.
