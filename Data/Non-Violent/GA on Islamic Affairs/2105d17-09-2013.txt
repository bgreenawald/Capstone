HH Sheikh Ammar bin Humaid Al Nuaimi, Crown Prince of Ajman, received HE Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, Awqaf's director general, the executive and Awqaf branch directors.
During the reception, Dr al Kaabi praised the unyielding support of HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, may Allah Protect him, his Deputy, Their highness the Rulers of the Emirates, HH Ruler of Ajman and HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces.
Dr al Kaabi briefed HH Sheikh Ammar of the regular meeting with Imams in Ajman and Umm Al Quwain, highlighting that Awqaf was keen to consolidate principles of moderation and wisdom in religious thought. He also brought into focus the role of mosques, which formidably keep pace with the modern civilisational and urban progress witnessed in the country.
HH Sheikh Ammar commended the efforts in building and maintaining mosques all over the UAE and the Official Fatwa Centre, which attends to thousands of questions from members of the public. This is evidence of the high level that Awqaf has reached in using modern technology and issuing documented Fatwas in accordance with religious rules, he said.
