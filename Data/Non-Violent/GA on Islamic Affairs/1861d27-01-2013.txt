Awqaf celebrated the victory of UAE football team in the Khaleeji 21 by organising a breakfast banquet for employees all over its branches in the Emirates. Executive directors, directors, heads of departments and all members of staff rejoiced this precious win.
On this occasion, Awqaf extends its greetings to HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, may Allah protect him, his Deputy HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, Their Highness Members of the Supreme Council, the Rulers of the Emirates, HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces and all UAE citizens and residents.
Winning the Khaleeji 21 is one more triumph for the UAE and its youth under the leadership of the wise leadership, which day after day is increasing our pride and belonging to this nation.
Awqaf also congratulated the national team for this great achievement which had brought joy and happiness all over the UAE, and lauded the efforts of all fans and supporters.
