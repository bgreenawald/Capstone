HH Sheikha Hana bint Juma Al Majid, Consort of HH Sheikh Saud bin Saqr Al Qasimi, Member of the Supreme Council and Ruler of Ras Al Khaimah, received a delegation of Awqaf female employees from the Emirate.
She expressed her delight regarding the development efforts initiated by Awqaf, which have benefitted mosques and strengthened principles of tolerance and moderation in the community. HH Sheikha Hana Al Majid highlighted various campaigns and events by Awqaf in Ras Al Khaimah, which are dedicated to serving mosques, Quranic centres and memorisation sessions. She also praised Awqaf's keen interest in developing Khateebs and providing holy Quran memorisation centres and sessions for population there.
She was briefed of Awqaf and its strategic plan, including programmes geared towards developing endowment projects, electronic systems for managing Waqf, Quran memorisation centres, mosques and Haj and Umra, as well as some services like prayer times in UAE, Fatwas, holy Quran memorisation sessions in summer and winter, among many.
