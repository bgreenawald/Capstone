Â The General Authority for Islamic Affairs and endowments (GAIAE) recently participated as a silver sponsor in the UAE International Special Needs Sports Conference.
The conference was held under the patronage of His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi, Deputy Supreme Commander of the Armed UAE forces. It was organized by UAE Special Needs Special Council, in addition to several local and international special needs organizations. Highly qualified experts in special needs shared their expertise in empowering people with special needs.
Dr.Mohammad AlKaabi, GAIAE director general, said that the conference was an opportunity to acquire knowledge about the best means to assimilate people with special needs into mainstream society. He added that" Our participation aims at highlighting the importance of sports in general, and for people with special needs in particular. Sports will enhance both the mental and physical skills of people with special aids and helps integrate them into society."
Dr.AlKaabi asserted that GAIAE's vision and strategic goals gear toward helping the development of society based on tolerant and moderate Islamic teachings. He also noted that GAIAE is eager to use endowment money in beneficial social activities.
