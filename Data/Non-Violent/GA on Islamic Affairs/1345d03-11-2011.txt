Hajj and Umrah operators' employees must have work permits. The purpose is to ensure that Hajj and Umrah operators are in compliance with legal requirements and that they provide the best services.






Department

Hajj and Umrah



Service Status

Active



Type of Service

Procedural 



Target audience


UAE Hajj and Umrah Operators




Service Accessibility



1-  Go to GAIAE website www.awqaf.gov.ae or open AWQAF smart App on mobile phones and tablets.
2-  Click on 'Our services' (Service Card).
3-  Click on 'Hajj and Umrah Services'.
4-  Click on 'Issue Work Permits for Hajj and Umrah Employees'.
5-  All information about the service will be available on the service card. To use the service, click on 'Go to Service'.
Users can also access the service through the links available on the homepage in the website and the AWQAF App.
 
Through the website:
1-  Go to GAIAE website www.awqaf.gov.ae
2-  Click on 'e-Services' on the homepage
3-  Click on 'Hajj and Umrah System'
4-  Enter your user ID and password
5-  Click on ' Issue Work Permits for Hajj and Umrah Employees'
6-  Fill the form
7- Send the application

Required Documents


 
- Copy of valid passport, ID card and residency permit for non-UAE citizens sponsored by the campaign
-Recent colour photo
-Service contract for positions including: (Administrator, physician, nurse, preacher)
-No objection letter from the employer for private sector for UAE nationals
 




Terms and Conditions


 
Application must have your full name, date and place of birth as mentioned in your passport
Original letter and a copy detailing the number of employees in charge
