The GAIAE's director general, Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, signed a contract with a construction company­­ to build a new mosque in the area of Al Salma 2 in Umm Al Quwain.
On this occasion Dr al Kaabi mentioned that building mosques receives great interest from HH Sheikh Khalifa, the UAE President, may Allah protect him, Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the UAE Vice President, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, Their Highness Members of the Supreme Council, and HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and the Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces.
He explained that the new mosque is part of the Mafhas al Qatat programme launched by the Awqaf, which is increasingly receiving donations from philanthropists from the UAE community .
The mosque will feature a mix of modern and heritage style of architecture and will offer space for about 1300 worshippers. It is expected It will be handed over by the second quarter of 2013.
