   The General Authority of Islamic Affairs and Endowments (GAIAE) signed at its headquarters in Abu Dhabi an agreement with Granada Architectural Consulting Engineers (GACE). According to the accord, the GACE will act as the consultant and supervisor of the construction of  the two Waqf projects that GAIAE will undertake in Asharj area and the city centre of  Al Ain. This move is in line with the directives  of the wise leadership, which holds great interest in Waqf investment programmes being a part  of its investment plans to serve the interests of  donors and achieve the purpose of Waqf.
The consulting agreement was signed by HE Dr. Mohammed Matar Salem Al Kaabi, the GAIAE's Director General, and GACE's representative. Dr. Al Kaabi said that building new Waqf projects is in accordance with the instructions of our astute leadership that call on the necessity to develop Waqf and is in line with the GAIAE's strategic plan as approved by the Cabinet.
Dr. Al Kaabi said that these investment programmes  also reflect the great economic development that UAE witnesses, which enable the GAIAE to fully carry out its role within the community.  On this occasion he praised the great support the Awqaf receives from HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the president of the UAE, HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the Vice President, Their Highness Members of the Supreme Council and Rulers of the Emirates, HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces.
