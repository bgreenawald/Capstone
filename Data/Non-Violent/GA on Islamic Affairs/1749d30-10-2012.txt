Awqaf's chairman, HE Dr Hamdan Musallam al Mazrouei issued a decision appointing a number of Emiratis in administrative positions in the Western Region branch offices.
Mr Sultan Zayed al Mazrouei, the branch director, said that this decision came to put into effect the wise directives of HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the President of UAE, may Allah protect him, HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the UAE Vice President, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, Their Highness Rulers of the Emirates, Members of the Supreme Court, HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces and HH Sheikh Hamdan bin Zayed, the Ruler's representative in the Western Region.
He added that this move was meant to support the Emiratisation process and offer employment opportunities for Emiratis. "More job interviews will also be held in the near future in order to fill other vacancies," Mr Sultan al Mazrouei said.
