HH Dr.Sheikh Sultan bin Khalifa Al Nahyan, the President's Advisor, opened Sheikha Hassa bint Mohammed Al Nahyan mosque in Al Sarouj area in Al-Ain city. Present during the inauguration were HE Dr.Matar Al Nuaimi, General Manager of  Al Ain Municipality and Mr.Abdul Aziz Al Ghaithi, Director of GAIAE office in Al Ain city as well as citizens and worshippers.
The mosque is designed to accommodate 1200 persons and is fully equipped to meet the needs of both worshippers and mosque personnel. Building cost was around 8 million dirhams and the construction spanned a year.
