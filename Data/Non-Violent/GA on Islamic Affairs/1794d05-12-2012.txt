Awqaf is celebrating the 41 anniversary of the UAE National Day in a ceremony at the Abu Dhabi theatre on November 27 at 9:00 am. There will be many activities, including speeches, poems, quizzes and prizes for winners. The ceremony is open for everyone to attend.
Awqaf is also planning other programmes across the country to celebrate this occasion, such as the Friday Khutba dedicated this week on the merits of the Union and the 15,200 sermons and lectures to be held in more than 5000 mosques. Their subjects will cover the religious principles of unity, love for the nation as prescribed in religious texts, aspects of unity and stability as well as agreement. Related topics will also include ways of achieving unity in our household and the community, the unity and the ways of establishing the nation.
Additionally, Awqaf prepared many booklets and religious awareness brochures and hundreds of thousands copies were printed and being distributed for free. Amongst these publications are the UAE from Creation to Empowerment, the Achievements of Awqaf under the Union, My Country is the UAE: Sense of Belonging and Loyalty.
On this occasion, Awqaf would like to extend its sincere congratulations to HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the UAE President, may Allah protect him, HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the UAE Vice President, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, Their Highness The Rulers of the Emirates and HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces, wishing to all UAE citizens and residents good health and success. Awqaf would also like to seize this opportunity to supplicate to Allah to have His mercy on the late founding fathers of this nation.
