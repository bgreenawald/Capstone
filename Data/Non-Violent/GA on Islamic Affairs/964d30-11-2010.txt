Upon arriving in Abu Dhabi Airport, H.E.Mohammad Obaid AlMazrouei, Chairman of the UAE Official Hajj Mission, expressed the utmost gratitude of the Mission's members for the continuous follow up and care of H.H. Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, UAE President, may Allah protect him, H.H. Sheikh Mohammad bin Rashed Al Maktoum, Vice President, Prime Minister, Ruler of Dubai, may Allah protect him and H.H.Sheikh Mohammad bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi, Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces.
H.E.Mr.AlMazrouei was pleased to announce that no serious health problems were reported this year " there were only few minor cases which were promptly treated" he added.
Mr.AlMazrouei noted that members of the Mission's committees were available in Jeddah Airport before the departure of the first pilgrims to facilitate the process. He added that the Saudi Airlines did not conform with the departure schedules of the additional flights resulting in flight delays. H.E. The Mission's Chairman lauded the UAE prudent leadership directives to resolve the issue and provide all the needs of the stranded pilgrims. He also commended the role of both the UAE Embassy and Consulate in Saudi Arabia in alleviating the pain of the pilgrims.
