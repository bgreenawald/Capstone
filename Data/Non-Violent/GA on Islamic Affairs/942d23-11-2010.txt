With a view to encourage new generations to read and memorize the Holy Quran, GAIAE department supervising religious centers and institutes holds an annual contest for memorizing the Holy Quran





Department

Religious Centers and Institutes



Service

Annual Contest for Memorizing the Holy Quran



Service Status

Active



Type of Service

Action needed



Target audience

All members of the community



Service Accessibility


 
Please log on to Awqaf website: www.awqaf.gov.ae .
Click on the holy Quran contest announcement and read the requirments. Applicants can apply in any of the Awqaf Quran Center in each Emirate

Required Documents


 
Passport copy
Two photos
 Sealed letter of enrollment in a center
Valid UAE residency permit for non-UAE citizens




 Terms and Conditions


Must be enrolled in one of the centers affiliated with GAIAE
Must have memorized the required parts of the Holy Quran
Must have good knowledge of Quran reciting (Tajweed)
Must pass the preliminary tests
Must nominate himself as a candidate in his center or any of GAIAE offices
Must not be employed by GAIAE or  a teacher of the Holy Quran
Must submit the required documents
