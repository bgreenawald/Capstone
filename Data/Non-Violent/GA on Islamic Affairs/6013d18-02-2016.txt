HH Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President, Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, honoured Awqaf, this year winner of the third edition of the Best m-Government Service Award at the national, Arab and global levels as well as the category of UAE university students. Awqaf was awarded the best government service at the closing ceremony of the World Government Summit held from February 8 to 10 at the Madinat Jumeirah in Dubai under the banner "Foreseeing future governments."
Sheikh Mohammed affirmed that the sheer number of entries at all levels reflected the success and the global dimension of the award. It also showed to what extent the world realised the importance of developing government services on innovative bases and evolving into into smart governments in the best interest of people, HH Sheikh Mohammed added.
"The award reflects the UAE's keen interest to set a ground-breaking regional and global example as evidenced by the large number of participants in the award from the Arab countries and elsewhere. This highlights the UAE's pioneering role in providing excellent government services and shows that the UAE has become a world-class destination in the field of government innovation," Sheikh Mohammed said, adding that the success of governments is gauged by the quality of services offered to their peoples.
Sheikh Mohammed honoured the winners of the award's ten categories at the national, Arab and global levels and the category of the UAE university students.
Nationally in the Social Affairs, the award went to AWQAF App. HE Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, Awqaf Chairman received the honorary shield
