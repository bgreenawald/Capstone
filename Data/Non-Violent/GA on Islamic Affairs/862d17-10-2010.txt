The strategic goals of the General Authority for Islamic Affairs and Endowments (GAIAE) are:

 
 
 
1- Contribute to efforts aimed at enhancing religious awareness, spreading Islamic culture and cementing the values of moderation, forbearance and cohesion in the community.  
2- Ensure offering a world-class religious services.
3- Ensure sustainability of Waqf fund raising and estate development in conformity with the Islamic objectives.
 4- Ensure all administrative services are provided in accordance with the standards of quality, efficiency and transparency. 
 5- Promote the culture of innovation at work.
