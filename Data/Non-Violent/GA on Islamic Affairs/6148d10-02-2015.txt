Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, Awqaf's Chairman, has affirmed that the speech delivered to the Government Summit by HH Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Supreme Deputy Commander of the UAE Armed Forces, has inspird the feelings of the young male and female citizens as it contained overwhelming feelings of patriotism, strategic depth and accurate foreseeing of the future.
"HH Sheikh Mohammed announced to the world that the UAE, which was founded by the late leader Sheikh Zayed Bin Sultan Al Nahyan, may Allah rest his soul, and his sincere brethren came to fruition and its citizens impressed the world," Dr al Kaabi said.
Dr al Kaabi added that the UAE President HH Sheikh Khalifa Bin Zayed Al Nahyan, HH Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, and their Highnesses the Rulers of the Emirates had put the locomotive of scientific, industrial, economic and technological progress on the road to prosperity and excellence.
