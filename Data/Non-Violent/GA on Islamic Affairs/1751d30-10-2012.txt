Awqaf's branch in the Western Region honoured recently the Imams partaking in the completion of the holy Quran during the holy month of Ramadan.
In a ceremony awarding Imams, Mr Sultan Zayed al Mazrouei, director of the branch, praised the unlimited support to Awqaf's projects from HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the President of the UAE, may Allah protect him, HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the UAE Vice President, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, Their Highness Rulers of the Emirates, Members of the Supreme Court, HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces and HH Sheikh Hamdan bin Zayed, the Ruler's representative in the Western Region.
Mr al Mazrouei mentioned that the completion of the holy Quran programme had received wide interest from worshippers, wishing it would continue to cover more mosques. On this occasion he thanked the participants for their valuable contribution to this programme.
