Awqaf inaugurated a new mosque named the Fatima Ali al Rumaithi Mosque in Al Taweelah area in the presence of Mr Mohammed Rashid al Fannan, HE Dr Hamdan Musallam al Mazrouei, Awqaf's chairman, HE Dr Mohammed Matar al Kaabi, Awqaf's director general, Mr Mohammed Obeid al Mazrouei, the executive director of Islamic affairs at Awqaf, Prof Dr Farouk Hamada, the religious advisor at the Abu Dhabi Crown Prince's Court, in addition to a host of local population.
The new mosque has a capacity of  300 worshippers.
On this occasion, Dr Hamdan al Mazrourei praised the special care devoted to the houses of Allah by HH Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed al Nahyan, the UAE President, may Allah protect him, his Deputy HH Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the UAE Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, Their Highness Members of the Supreme Council, the Rulers of the Emirates, HH General Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces.
HE also underscored their judicial directives aimed at enhancing mosques and promoting their leading position in the community, especially their role in highlighting the civilised image of moderate Islamic traditions. Dr Hamdan al Mazrouei further lauded the unlimited support of the UAE wise leadership to Awqaf and the positive response of philanthropists to "Mafhas al Qatah" campaigns, an initiative led by Awqaf for building and maintaining mosques.
